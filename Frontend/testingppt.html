The Challenge: Problem Statement

The Current Scenario

Pomegranate farmers rely on **manual, physical inspection** for disease detection, a process that is slow, highly subjective, and error-prone.

Critical Risk & Economic Impact

Failure to quickly identify critical diseases (like **Alternaria, Bacterial Blight, Cercospora, and Anthracnose**) leads to rapid **crop loss** and severe economic impact.

Project Objectives & Deliverables

  • Primary Objective: High-Accuracy AI System
    To develop and deploy a **ResNet34-based** AI system that accurately classifies the four major pomegranate diseases and healthy status (Target Accuracy: $95\%+$).
  • Deliverable 1: User-Friendly Accessibility
    Design and implement a **user-friendly web interface** for easy, non-technical use by farmers in the field.
  • Deliverable 2: Dual Detection Functionality
    Provide two main modes for diagnosis: **Live Webcam Feed** (for real-time assessment) and **Static Image Upload**.

Methodology: The AI Pipeline

1. Data Sourcing & Management

  • Images gathered from **Kaggle** and supplemented with specific samples from **Google Images**.
  • Utilized the **Roboflow** platform for efficient dataset preprocessing, resizing, and augmentation.

2. Model Training & Selection

  • Employed the **ResNet34 (Residual Network)** architecture for robust feature extraction and classification.
  • Training was executed via the **Roboflow platform**, leveraging powerful Python-based deep learning libraries.

3. Deployment & Integration

The trained ResNet34 model is hosted via the **Roboflow API**. Our custom **FastAPI** server handles the secure connection and result formatting for the farmer.
